About Us

English Universe Academy


Learn English online, from the comfort of your home and at your own pace with our language courses for learners of all ages.

Whether you are looking for beginner or advanced English lessons, our courses are a great fit for you.

At English Universe our aim is to teach English effectively with a single unique English course which has been designed for beginners and intermediate and advanced learners alike.

The course is conducted online as well as at reputed educational institutions in Colombo Horana Mathugama and kalutara.

Students will experience a simple learning method that will increase their knowledge steadily in a consistent manner.
